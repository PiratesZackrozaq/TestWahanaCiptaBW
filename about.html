About us

PT. Wahanacipta Bangunwisma founded in 1994 is one of well-known national consultant firms domiciled in Jakarta. The Firm has fast experience in providing various consulting services and supported with professional and experienced engineers as well as strong management. PT. Wahanacipta Bangunwisma has been successful to implement various consulting services nationwide for both public and private sectors. Since early 2019. the Firm have been carrying out a number of industrial buildings. hospitals. and housings. the services of which cover Detailed Engineering Design (DED}, Construction Supervision. Topographic Survey and Soil Investigation. Feasibility Study, and Audit of Building Structure.
